How It Works

Capturing the perfect screenshot is easier than ever with our intuitive workflow.

1

Trigger EasyShot

Press the global hotkey (default: Print Screen) or select from the system tray to activate EasyShot capture mode from anywhere.

2

Select Area

Click and drag to select the exact portion of the screen you want to capture. Use the crosshair for pixel-perfect precision.

3

Edit (Optional)

Use the popup toolbar to add annotations, highlights, text, or blur sensitive information before saving your screenshot.

4

Save or Share

Click Save to store locally, or use the Share button to upload and get an instant shareable link copied to your clipboard.
